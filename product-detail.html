Description


Forget about fumbling around while trying to close your bottle of powdered ammonia after taking a big wiff.

Overdrive is a liquid formula that we designed to be safer, longer lasting, and harder hitting than the others. The flip top cap allows for easy opening and closing, while still being leak proof.

What makes Overdrive safer than other products?

The majority of ammonia inhalant manufacturers use ammonium chloride as their compound of choice to produce the ammonia gas that you inhale. What they don't disclose (or fail to realize) is that ammonium chloride decomposes into both ammonia and hydrogen chloride. Upon inhallation, hydrogen chloride gas forms corrosive hydrochloric acid on contact with water found in body tissue. To put it simply, you don't want that in your ammonia inhalant product.

Overdrive produces pure ammonia gas; the stuff you actually want to inhale in order to iniate the intense focus and that crucial fight-or-flight reflex required during max effort lifts.
